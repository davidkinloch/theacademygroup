Academy Companies & Partners

The Academy is built on a sustainable business model that aligns the commercial and social sectors.

We take an ownership stake in a diverse portfolio of commercial companies. The earned revenues from these investments fund our education programs while Academy Companies provide apprenticeship opportunities for students in high school and college, and guarantees employment to Academy graduates.

Meet the Real Assets of The Academy

The Academy is made up of young people ages 8 to 22 with one thing in common; extraordinary talent with a clear path to become the leaders and innovators of tomorrow.

Dominique Taylor

Dominique Taylor is entering her senior year of high school at UChicago Charter School Woodlawn. She currently has a 3.94 GPA and is interested in applying to schools like The University of Chicago, University of Illinois Urbana Champaign, Howard, and University of California Riverside. Dominique is a Youth Leader and active member of her church, and does regular volunteer work to provide meals to members of her local community. Dominique also operated her own radio station in Bronzeville.

“I want to be a Chief Financial Officer. I'd like to get as much education as possible, so I can improve my chances in life, and become a successful leader. My faith is central to who I am. I believe in speaking things into being. I am excited to work with The Academy Group as I continue to relentlessly pursue my ambitions.”

Dominique Taylor

Brieanna McCann

Brieanna McCann is entering her senior year of high school at Kenwood Academy. She has a 4.0 GPA and a 1320 SAT composite score. She is interested in applying to schools like USC, Pomona, UCLA, and Stanford. Brieanna is active in her school’s Latin Club and National Honors Society. Brieanna has a deep love for Captain America and is excited to take a film studies course this fall.

“I am really looking forward to this fall because I am going to be able to visit some of the universities I am applying to. I have never been able to visualize myself in a college setting and I can’t wait to actually be standing on these campuses and know I will be a student at one of them next year.”

Brieanna McCann

Carlos Elosia

Carlos Eloisa is entering his senior year at Walter Payton College Prep. He currently has a 3.4 GPA and is interested in applying to schools like DePauw, University of Illinois Urbana Champaign, University of Wisconsin-Madison, Pomona, Occidental, and Boston College.
